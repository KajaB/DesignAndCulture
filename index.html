My Mission Statement

What I care about most in design is that i can bring a piece of me into my work and share it with a wide range of people. I can share what I care and stand for, or share what I enjoy, to reach out to others who share those same things. My work topics can be from social issues to topics about improving the environment. Some design values that I think about when designing are aesthetic, social, and environmental. These design values support my personal values because with aesthetics the design would be showing the nature and appreciation of beauty. This is where aesthetic design would share my first personal value of wanting to make work that last long, designs that someone would want to keep and hold onto. For social, one would be creating design in a n attempt to address particular social issues using art and creativity. This supports my second personal value of want to make work that makes a difference. Lastly, environmental design would be creating works that mean to make a statement on environmental issues, which supports my final personal value of doing work that is collaborative, because with environmental issues it takes everyone as a whole to make a change or improve the environment and the work would be pushing towards getting everyone to help out together.
